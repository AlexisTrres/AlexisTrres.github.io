Welcome to Few Seconds Math

This game will challenge your mathematical skills against the clock.

You can set the difficulty by selecting the different operators and the maximum number you want to play with.

The most difficult operations, the most score you will achieve!

For each correct answer, a few seconds will be added to the clock, depending on the level you choosed.

The game ends when the time is over.

Good luck!
